## About

Working on Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) optimization and innovation, an MSE x ECE collaboration between Garten Lab and the Plasma and Dielectrics Laboratory.

## What I Do

- Fabricate and cold-sinter metal oxide varistor samples for high-voltage DC power applications
- Conduct physical property tests (density, hardness) and electrical measurements (impedance, IV curves) to evaluate material performance
- Focus on optimizing microstructure to strengthen intergranular phases and increase potential barriers, improving voltage handling capacity

## Earlier Work (Spring 2024)

Worked on piezoelectric amplifier design involving analog circuits, PCB design, and SPICE simulation.
